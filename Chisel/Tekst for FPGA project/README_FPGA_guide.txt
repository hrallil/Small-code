Altera Cyclone V SoC development kit (SX) 

----Running the FPGA----
When unpacking the FPGA, follow the steps in the -- Quick start guide--. when done, an ip address should show on the board, and access to LED and display control should be available.

NOTE, when setting up the Ethernet port the ethernet cable should be plugged into J2. To connect our pc to the system a splitter is needed.
